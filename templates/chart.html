Crime Statistics

June and July were the highest reported crime months of 2018 (both at 9.2%). Feb was the lowest at 6.9%, possibly because there are less days in Feb and it is one of the colder months.

No surprise here, 70% of crime was committed by males (29% female, 1% unknown).

Top 3 crimes in 2018 were: Aggravated assault, stealing and non-aggravated assault

Most frequent age range: 30-40

15% of all crime was committed with a firearm.
