Crime Data - KC

The Kansas City police department (KCPD) has given us the task to use our data science skills to analyze crime data from 2018. They have a ton of crime data they want to make use of and need some help making sense of it. They want to know which troublesome neighborhoods need more attention.

They have their hands full keeping up with all the incidents that happen in the surrounding areas as is, so they would like for us to come up with an easy and interactive way for them to be able to look at the data and make important decisions on which troublesome neighborhoods need more attention.
